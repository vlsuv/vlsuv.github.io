The application allows you to make an independent appointment with a doctor, both an adult and a child, see the history of visits.

Vita Card allows you to save time and eliminate the need to visit clinics to make an appointment. Besides there is an opportunity to get referential information about all medical organizations in the region, their contacts and addresses, and also you can find out by your residential address to which clinics you concern. 

Convenient search for medical institutions on the map.

## Main Featured
- Make an appointment with a doctor online in a few clicks
- The ability to make doctor’s appointments for relative
- Search by clinics
- Viewing clinics in a list and on a map
- Filters for clinics  
- The latest news of medical organizations
- The ability to see clinics’ reviews and leave yours
- View current recipes
- View attachments to a doctor or clinic
- View all cards’ record
- Make reminders of appointment’s durations and frequency  
- The ability to contact technical support
